My Hobbies!

Spikeball

I've been playing Spikeball for almost six years, and joined the competitive team right when I got to Michigan. I feel like in such a big university, it brings me a smaller sense of community, which I now even have a leadership position in!

Baseball

As a four year old, I did not realize how much passion I would find for baseball. However, I ended up playing all the way through high school and gaining many life leadership skills from it.

Basketball

While I found basketball later than a lot of people I played with, I still very much enjoyed the hustle. I find that it is one of my favorite games to play with friends in college.

Sleep

As ironic as it sounds to say, sleep gives me such a boost and motivation to shape my days. Throughout 2023, I have been setting goals to ensure I get 56 hours of sleep every week!

Geoguessr

Despite how niche of a hobby it is, I have come to really love the game of Geoguessr. I feel like I have learned so much about the world and how much I want to explore and travel when I am older.

Chess

During the height of Covid, I watched a lot of people play chess online, and decided I really wanted to learn. While I'm still not the best, I enjoy the mental battle that comes from the game.

Lifting Weights

Like sleep, going to the gym is something I really like planning my days around. I really feel like I can see physical and mental progress, especially on days where I lack motivation.

Volleyball

Vacations, retreats, intermurals, and university clubs are among the places I have learned the game of volleyball and really started to enjoy it. One day my jump serve will be perfect!

Card Magic

Also during the height of Covid, I dove into the world of magic and really liked the spontenaity of card magic. I still have my main trick mastered and love to perform it anytime!

Cooking

While it's not my strongest skill, I enjoy cooking the meals I have mastered. I have become known among my friends for my specialty - chocolate chip pancakes.
